Geological Timescales

Geological timescales are a system used by scientists to better understand the History of our Planet. The fundamental unit of geological timescales is the Period. A group of Periods make an Era (such as the Mesozoic) which, in turn, can be grouped together into Eons (Phanerozoic, for example).

You can read about each time period at there respective pages.
